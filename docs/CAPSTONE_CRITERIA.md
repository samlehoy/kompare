# AI-Powered Smart Shopping Assistant & Product Decision Engine

This project aims to develop an AI-powered shopping assistant that enhances the online shopping experience by providing users with intelligent product comparisons, personalized recommendations, and multimodal product understanding. The system leverages advanced AI techniques, including context engineering, prompt engineering, and multimodal AI, to analyze product information, compare options based on user preferences, and generate structured recommendations.

The project focuses on building a functional localhost application that demonstrates the potential of AI in improving decision-making for online shoppers. The shopping assistant will analyze product details, compare options, and generate personalized recommendations, all without relying on an external database.

> **Category:** E-Commerce  
> **Duration:** 6 Weeks

---

## Problem Statement

Online shoppers often struggle to make informed purchasing decisions due to the overwhelming amount of product information and the difficulty in comparing options effectively. This project addresses this challenge by developing an AI-powered shopping assistant that leverages advanced AI techniques to analyze product details, provide personalized recommendations, and ultimately improve the decision-making process for online shoppers.

---

# Objectives

- Apply context engineering for product comparison and recommendations.
- Design multimodal AI systems (image + text inputs).
- Use structured prompts for consistent outputs.
- Implement context pruning for large product descriptions.
- Build multi-turn conversational assistants with memory.
- Integrate AI reasoning into real-world decision-making systems.

# Milestones

## Week 1 — Product Data Acquisition and Preprocessing
Gather product data from various online sources and preprocess it for AI model training and inference.

## Week 2 — Structured Prompt Design for Product Comparison
Develop structured prompts that enable the AI model to compare products effectively and generate UI-friendly comparison reports.

## Week 3 — Context Pruning for Long Product Descriptions
Implement context pruning techniques to summarize long product descriptions and extract key information.

## Week 4 — Multimodal Prompting for Image-Based Product Understanding
Develop multimodal prompts that combine image and text inputs to enable the AI model to understand products based on visual and textual information.

## Week 5 — Conversational Shopping Assistant with Multi-Turn Context
Build a conversational shopping assistant that can maintain user preferences and provide personalized recommendations based on multi-turn conversations.

## Week 6 — Integration and Deployment
Integrate all the components of the AI-powered shopping assistant into a functional localhost application and deploy it for testing and evaluation.
